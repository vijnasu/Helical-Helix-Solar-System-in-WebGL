Introduction

The project “Helical Helix Solar System”, a representative view of Solar System in a new perspective. Static model cannot describe the dynamic nature. The Sun is moving with certain speed. All the planets follow the Sun in identical Helical Orbits. The solid angle at the source of light and the distances to each planet are maintained uniformly.


WebGl is the tool used to implement the 3D view of new interpretation of Solar system. An abstraction of WebGL called as THREE.js has been used for implementation.
